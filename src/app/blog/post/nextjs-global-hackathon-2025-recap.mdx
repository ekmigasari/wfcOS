The [Next.js Global Hackathon 2025](https://next-hackathon-2025.vercel.app/) has concluded, marking another milestone in showcasing developer ingenuity worldwide. The event, culminating in a live-streamed awards ceremony hosted by Lee Robinson (VP of Developer Experience at Vercel), celebrated exceptional projects built with Next.js, with a strong emphasis on performance, design, and cutting-edge AI integration.

## Hackathon Highlights: Pushing Boundaries

Participants were challenged across three main categories: **Highest Quality**, **Fastest App**, and **Best Use of AI**. The goal was to encourage developers to push the boundaries of what's possible with Next.js, from meticulous design and performance optimization to innovative AI-driven solutions.

The competition was fierce, but several projects stood out for their excellence and innovation:

### Overall Winner: Text0

Emerging as the grand prize winner, **Text0** was recognized for its outstanding design, remarkable performance, and intuitive user experience. This AI-powered writing assistant masterfully leverages Next.js capabilities to provide a seamless and powerful platform for content creation. The Text0 team has earned a grand prize trip to San Francisco! Text0 also impressed with its sophisticated AI integration, earning it the **Best Use of AI** award.

### Highest Quality App: Work From Coffee OS

Impressing the judges with its exceptional user experience, performance, and code quality, **Work From Coffee OS (WFCOS)** secured the award for **Highest Quality App**. This project offers a unique operating system experience meticulously tailored for remote work environments, enhancing productivity and focus.

- Learn more about [WFCOS's hackathon achievement](./wfcos-hackathon-award-announcement.mdx).
- Dive deeper into the [Work From Coffee OS features and vision](./nextjs-hackathon-highest-quality-apps.mdx).

### Fastest App: gitfaster

**gitfaster** earned its title by showcasing exceptional performance. This application delivered rapid load times and highly efficient workflows, perfectly demonstrating the power of Next.js in building high-speed, responsive applications.
